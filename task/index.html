Your Questions, Answered

Are KGiSL Microcollege job-readiness courses online or offline?

KGiSL MicroCollege offers flexible options, providing both online and on-campus(offline) certification courses to cater to diverse preferences of our students.

How long does the upskilling courses take to complete?

Our upskilling courses typically take 5-6 months.

What's involved in the "KAT test"?

KAT is an AON assessment to identify your skill levels in your chosen domain. It helps us identify and map the 'right' course for you to launch your career.

What is the eligibility criteria for admission?

Eligibility criteria differ by course; we value a passion for learning and upskilling. We recommend completing your graduation before joining, aligning with employer preferences.

Will I earn a certificate?

Yes! Upon course and project completion, you'll receive course completion certificate and industry readiness certificate. You can leverage this certificate on LinkedIn, displaying verified skills to potential employers.
